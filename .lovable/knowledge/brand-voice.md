# Hells Canyon Designs — Brand Voice & Positioning

## Identity
Custom decorated apparel shop in Lewiston, Idaho. Services: leather patch hats, embroidery, screen printing, and DTF.

## Voice
- **Confident, direct, and human**
- Always "we" — never "I"
- No corporate jargon, no fluff

## Brand Promise: "We Got You"
- We catch problems before customers know they exist
- We own outcomes
- We treat every project like it matters

## Positioning
- **Not the cheapest option** — and we don't pretend to be
- **Not set up for single-item orders** — we're a production shop
- **Best customers**: businesses and brands who want a long-term partner, not a transaction
- We are craftspeople, not a print-on-demand kiosk
