

# Hells Canyon Designs — Lead Generation Website

## Overview
A modern, professional website reimagined as a **lead-generation engine** for Hells Canyon Designs' custom services. No retail store — just a clear, compelling path from "I need custom gear" to "Here's my quote request." The gang sheet builder will live on your Shopify store (using a Shopify app), and we'll link to it from the DTF page.

---

## Page 1: Homepage — "Your Brand, Our Craft"
- **Hero section** with bold tagline, high-quality background imagery of your work, and a prominent CTA: **"Get a Free Quote"**
- **Four service cards** linking to dedicated pages:
  - 🧢 Custom Leather Patch Hats
  - 🧵 Embroidery
  - 🖨️ Screen Printing
  - 🔥 DTF Transfers & Garments *(NEW!)*
- **Social proof section** — star rating, review count, rotating testimonials
- **"How It Works"** — 3-step visual: Tell Us What You Need → Get Your Custom Quote → We Make It Happen
- **Portfolio gallery** — best examples across all services
- **FAQ section** — turnaround times, minimums, artwork requirements

## Page 2: Custom Leather Patch Hats
- Visual showcase of patch types: Laser Engraved Leather, UV Printed, Embroidered
- Hat brand options (Richardson 112, YP Classics, etc.) with photos
- Trust callout: "4,000+ hats in stock"
- **Guided Quote Builder** (multi-step form):
  1. Patch type → 2. Hat style & colors → 3. Quantity range → 4. Upload artwork → 5. Timeline → 6. Contact info

## Page 3: Embroidery Services
- Portfolio of embroidery work — hats, jackets, polos, workwear
- Capabilities overview
- **Guided Quote Builder**:
  1. Item type → 2. Quantity → 3. Embroidery locations → 4. Upload artwork → 5. Timeline & contact

## Page 4: Screen Printing
- Equipment showcase (ROQ P-14XL, CTS technology)
- Examples of printed work
- **Guided Quote Builder**:
  1. Garment type → 2. Print colors → 3. Print locations → 4. Quantity → 5. Upload artwork → 6. Timeline & contact

## Page 5: DTF Transfers & Garments
- Explain what DTF is and its advantages (full color, works on many fabrics, photo-quality)
- Two clear paths:
  - **Custom DTF Garments** — "We print it for you" with a guided quote builder
  - **Gang Sheet Builder** — Prominent button/link that takes customers to your **Shopify store** where the gang sheet builder app lives (direct ordering of transfer sheets)
- **Guided Quote Builder** for custom DTF garments:
  1. Finished garments or loose transfers? → 2. Garment type → 3. Size & quantity → 4. Upload artwork → 5. Timeline & contact

## Page 6: About / Our Story
- Your background, the Lewiston Idaho shop, the team
- Photos of the shop floor, equipment, team at work
- "White glove service" and local craftsmanship messaging

## Shared Components
- **Sticky header** — logo, service navigation links, phone number, and a "Get a Quote" button always visible
- **Footer** — contact info, social links (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube), location, quick quote CTA
- **Mobile-first responsive design** — optimized for social media traffic on phones
- **Success notifications** after form submissions

## Design Direction
- Dark, rugged color scheme (dark charcoal/navy with gold/amber accents from your logo)
- Clean, modern layout with generous white space and large imagery
- Professional but approachable brand voice
- Premium typography that matches the quality of your work

## Technical Approach
- All quote forms collect submissions locally with success confirmations (ready to connect to email/CRM via Supabase later)
- Gang sheet ordering links out to your Shopify store — keeping Shopify as a focused tool for that one direct-order feature
- No cart, checkout, or retail catalog on this site
- Optimized for fast loading and SEO
